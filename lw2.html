EDUCATING THE WHOLE PERSON

Vilnius Gediminas Technical University’s educational mission is to be the leader in technology educaation, which also means encouraging students to broaden their horizon. We provide every opportunity for students to seek knowledge and develop their unique talents, whether intellectual or physical.

Clubs and activities

A dynamic university is an open and creative university. We are proud of our energetic, enthusiastic, talented students, and encourage them to further develop their skills at the University.

VILNIUS TECH has several art clubs that help to sustain long and historic traditions. Recently, our clubs celebrated their 50th anniversaries.

The most significant and oldest of these clubs include theatre, choir and dance. In our theatre studio, “Palėpė”, students act, direct and create, and in our mixed academic choir, “Gabija”, they sing together and perform in public to appreciative audiences. The folk dance ensemble, “Vingis”, celebrates native Lithuanian dance and is accompanied by traditional folk instruments.


VILNIUS TECH sports

For sports men and women there is no shortage of opportunities. Our athletes compete in sports as diverse as basketball, volleyball, football, tennis players, wrestling, track and field athletes, power lifting, and many other sports. VILNIUS TECH athletes are well represented in inter-university, national, international, European and World championships and tournaments.

Beyond the University, Vilnius has plenty of sports and entertainment venues to suit all interests.
